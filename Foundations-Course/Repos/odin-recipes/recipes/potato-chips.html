Homemade Potato Chips

Description

Making your own Homemade Potato Chips from scratch is easier than you think and so rewarding. It may take a little bit of time, but once you know how to make your own potato chips, you'll know it's worth the extra work!

Ingredients

  • Potato
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Get a mandoline and then slice your potatoes super thin!
  2. Soak the potatoes in an ice bath.
  3. Also key to ultimate Homemade Potato Chip crispiness - drain the potatoes and pat dry.
  4. Fry in hot oil.
  5. No matter what you do with them, (fold or leave flat), after a few minutes and they begin to brown, remove them from the hot oil and let drain on a paper towel-lined plate, in a single layer, and then sprinkle with salt.
  6. Enjoy!
